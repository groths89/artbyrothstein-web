About the Artist

Barry Rothstein

He's a native of New York City, and has made St. Petersburg, Florida his home for the past 30 years and counting. While in New York City he has studied Drawing, Art, Illustration, and Design at the City University of New York (CUNY), The Parsons School of Art and Design, School of Visual Arts, and The Fashion Institute of Technology. During his time in Florida he has studied at the Arts Center of St. Petersburg.

A contributing artist to several magazines, Uncle Barry's works have been widely exhibited at numerous galleries and art shows in New York, Atlanta, and Florida. His most notable exhibitions include Sothby's Auction Galleries, Washington Square Art Exhibit, Fresh Meadows Art Show, Atlantic City Boardwalk Art Exhibition, and the Ritz-Carlton Atlanta. At the Washington Square Art Exhibit he won an award for his Pen & Ink/Etching work.

In his local area in Florida nis works have been exhibited at Artrageous Gallery, Adrian Howard Gallery, Paradise Gallery, Fiorini Gallery, TW Curtis Gallery, Pinellas County Fair Art Show, The Arts Center, and the Tampa Pride Art Exhibit. Two of these shows brought him four different awards and ribbons. At the Pinellas County Fair Art Show he received two awards. Also, at the Tampa Pride Art Exhibit he was awarded two ribbons.

Uncle Barry's work was selected as one of 20 local artists in the Tampa area for the 2007 Westshore Expose Art Project. He has been represented and on display at the Finn Gallery and can be currently seen at the offices of Executive Reporting Service in Clearwater where he had a successful show November of 2011. Uncle Barry is also represented worldwide on Fine Art America.

Uncle Barry was featured in the Tampa Bay Magazine July/August issue 2006.

In May of 2012, Uncle Barry was accepted as a member of the Pastel Society of America, a juried organization and an elite grup of artists. Uncle Barry received a Merit Award at the Pastel Society of Tampa Bay Art Show on February 13, 2015 for "Vegas Sky".

Many of Barry Rothstein's works may be found in numerous provate collections worldwide including big names like Sir Elton John.

He is a member of several Boards including being Past President of the St. Petersburg Downtown Business Association. He is also the Director of Business Development for Consonant Custom Media and is active in World Ventures/Dreamtrips when he is not working on his art.

Uncle Barry recently started in the Real Estate industry down in Florida selling million dollar houses.
